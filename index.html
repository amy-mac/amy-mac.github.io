About Me

Greetings! My name is Amy MacKinnon and I’m a web developer, thespian, nerd, and Jill of All Trades.

I was born and raised in the San Francisco Bay Area, but have also lived in Idaho, Colorado, and Virginia before returning home. I've worked in a few different fields and as Xena would say, "I have many skills." My greatest passion in life is theatre, followed by computers & technology. I’m doing lots of things I love and enjoying myself every step of the way.

Other interests and hobbies include: baking pies, taking care of my cats, tap dancing, being a new parent, and many other interests that keep my time quite filled. I'm extremely fascinated by just about everything out there in the world. This website highlights only a small part of my adventures.
